О Mavi

Hi! My name is Mavi, and I am a digital artist. I love creating vivid, dreamy images inspired by the night sky, moonlight, and fantasy worlds. I believe that art is a way to express your feelings and share them with others. I like to work with deep, rich colors: pink, purple and blue — they seem to transport me to another world. Each drawing is a new journey for me, and I am happy to share it with you.

I'm just starting out, but I have so many ideas and projects! I would like my works to inspire you as much as they inspire me to create them.

Mavi Blogs
