About LUMOO

We're on a mission to transform spaces through innovative lighting solutions that combine cutting-edge technology with timeless design.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, LUMOO began with a simple idea: to create lighting solutions that not only illuminate spaces but transform them. Our journey started in a small workshop where we experimented with different materials and technologies.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of lighting innovation, creating products that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal, and sustainability with smart technology.

Quality
